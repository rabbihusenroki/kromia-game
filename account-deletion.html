Delete Your KROMIA Account

What Data Will Be Deleted

Upon account deletion, the following information will be permanently removed:

  • Your complete user profile
  • XP points and level progress
  • Streak data and daily login records
  • Inventory items and unlocked titles
  • Friends list and social connections
  • Challenge history and match records
  • Game settings and preferences
  • Leaderboard entries
⚠️ Cannot Be Undone

This deletion is permanent and irreversible. Make sure you want to proceed before submitting your request.

Temporary Data Retention

Some records may be retained temporarily for the following purposes:

  • Legal Compliance: Required by law to maintain certain records
  • Fraud Prevention: To prevent abuse and protect other users
  • Backup Systems: Automated backups may retain data briefly
  • Security Logs: System logs for security monitoring
⏱️ Retention Period

Temporary records will be deleted within 30 days of your deletion request being processed.

⏰ Standard Processing Time

Requests are typically processed within 30 business days. You will receive a confirmation email once the deletion is complete.

Consider These Alternatives

Before deleting your account, you might want to consider:

  • Temporary Break: Simply uninstall the app instead of deleting data
  • Data Export: Contact us to export your game statistics first
  • Privacy Settings: Adjust your privacy settings within the app
  • Contact Support: Discuss any concerns with our team first
