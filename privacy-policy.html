1 Information We Collect

1.1 Information You Provide

When you sign in with Google, we collect:

  • Your Google account display name
  • Your Google profile photo URL
  • Your Google account email address (used for authentication only)
  • Your selected display name and country (entered during onboarding)

1.2 Information Collected Automatically

When you use the App, we automatically collect:

  • Game scores and results
  • Streak data and XP progress
  • Leaderboard rankings
  • Device information (operating system, device type)
  • App usage data (game sessions, features used)
  • IP address (collected by Firebase automatically)

1.3 Information from Third Parties

We receive information from:

  • Google Sign-In (authentication data)
  • Google Firebase (data storage and analytics)
  • Google AdMob (advertising data)

2 How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Create and manage your game account
  • Display your name and profile on leaderboards
  • Track your game progress, XP, streaks, and rankings
  • Enable multiplayer and friend challenge features
  • Show relevant advertisements through Google AdMob
  • Improve the App and fix technical issues
  • Respond to your requests and support needs

3 How We Share Your Information

3.1 Public Information

The following information is visible to other players:

  • Your display name
  • Your profile photo
  • Your leaderboard scores and rankings
  • Your equipped title badges
  • Your country flag

3.2 Service Providers

We share data with trusted service providers:

  • Google Firebase (Firestore database, Authentication, Analytics)
  • Google AdMob (advertising)

These providers process data on our behalf and are bound by their own privacy policies.

3.3 We Do Not Sell Your Data

✓ Your Privacy Matters

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to any third party.

4 Google AdMob and Advertising

The App uses Google AdMob to display advertisements including:

  • Banner ads (shown on the home screen)
  • Interstitial ads (shown between game sessions)
  • Rewarded ads (optional, for in-game benefits)
⚠️ Personalized Ads

AdMob may collect and use data to show personalized ads based on your interests. You can opt out of personalized ads in your device settings.

For more information about how Google uses data:

https://policies.google.com/privacy

5 Data Retention

We retain your data as long as your account is active.

Account Deletion

If you delete your account through the App settings, your personal data will be permanently deleted from our Firebase database within 30 days.

Note: Game statistics may be retained in anonymized form for leaderboard integrity purposes.

6 Children's Privacy

The App is rated for users aged 3 and above on Google Play.

⚠️ Important Notice for Parents

We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 years of age without verifiable parental consent.

If you believe we have collected information from a child under 13 without consent, please contact us immediately at the email address below and we will delete such information.

However, no method of transmission over the Internet is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security.
